Greece

Why Greece Is Special to Me

Greece is where ancient history meets Mediterranean charm. From the philosophical birthplace of democracy to sun-drenched islands dotted with white-washed buildings, every corner tells a story spanning millennia.

How to Get There

Flight Routes

  • Manila (MNL) to Athens (ATH)
  • Common stops: Dubai or Istanbul
  • Flight time: 15-20 hours total
  • Best airlines: Emirates, Turkish

Travel Requirements

  • Schengen Visa needed
  • Valid for 6 months after exit
  • Travel insurance required
  • Book flights 3-6 months ahead
