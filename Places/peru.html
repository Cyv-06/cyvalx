Peru

Why Peru Is Special to Me

Peru is a land of incredible contrasts, where ancient Incan civilization meets modern South American culture. From the mysterious ruins of Machu Picchu to the Amazon rainforest, and from the Andean highlands to the Pacific coast, every region offers unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

Cultural Highlights

Cuisine

  • Ceviche - fresh seafood dish
  • Lomo Saltado - stir-fried beef
  • Pisco Sour - national drink
  • Anticuchos - street food favorite

Traditions

  • Inca festivals
  • Traditional weaving
  • Andean music
  • Sacred ceremonies

Cusco

Ancient capital of the Inca Empire, featuring stunning colonial architecture, traditional markets, and gateway to Machu Picchu

Lima

Peru's vibrant capital, known for its world-class cuisine, historic center, and beautiful coastal views
