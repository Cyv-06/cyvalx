Japan

Why Japan Is Special to Me

Japan is a fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity. From peaceful temples and traditional tea ceremonies to bustling cities and technological marvels, every experience here is unique and memorable.

How to Get There

Flight Routes

  • Manila (MNL) to Tokyo (NRT/HND)
  • Cebu (CEB) to Osaka (KIX)
  • Direct flight time: 4-5 hours
  • Airlines: PAL, Cebu Pacific, AN

Travel Requirements

  • Tourist visa required
  • Valid passport (6 months)
  • Return ticket proof
  • Hotel reservations

Osaka

Japan's kitchen capital, famous for its street food culture, vibrant Dotonbori district, and friendly locals

Kyoto

The cultural heart of Japan with over 1,600 temples, traditional geisha districts, and peaceful zen gardens
