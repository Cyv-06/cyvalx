Iceland

Why Iceland Is Special to Me

Iceland is a land of fire and ice, where nature puts on its most spectacular shows. From the mesmerizing Northern Lights to powerful geysers, from black sand beaches to massive glaciers, every corner of this island nation feels like stepping into another world.

How to Get There

Flight Routes

  • Manila (MNL) to Keflavík (KEF)
  • Common stops: London or Frankfurt
  • Total flight time: 18-24 hours
  • Airlines: Qatar Airways, KLM

Travel Requirements

  • Schengen Visa required
  • Travel insurance needed
  • Valid passport (6 months)
  • Proof of accommodation

Reykjavik

Iceland's charming capital, where colorful buildings meet modern design, featuring vibrant arts, cuisine, and nightlife scenes.

Vatnajökull

Europe's largest glacier, offering ice caves, hiking trails, and stunning views of Iceland's raw natural beauty.
